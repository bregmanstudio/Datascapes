Sources of Data About Society

  1. Matthew Jackon’s data page. Lots of economic data for a variety of (excellent) papers. Includes a great social network data set – complete social relation data for 40+ Indian villages.
  2. Correlates of War: Lots of different types of data related to conflict and armed interventions. Includes general markers like economic and social markers as well (for example bilateral trade data).
  3. Voteview: Keith Poole’s page with US legislative roll call data.
  4. Voeten’s UN roll call data.
  5. 300, mostly small, social science data sets. Includes stripped out social networks from the AdHealth waves. Also, almost all the “classic” networks – C Elegans, Zachary’s karate club, etc.
  6. A variety of social science data sources. Many are not network data per se, but some of it is.
  7. CRCNS data page (neuroscience).
  8. More brain data links.
  9. Mark Newman’s data page.
